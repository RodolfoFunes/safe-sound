Mission Statement

Our community will ensure that all citizens and visitors will feel safe and aware of the evens in their community by using voice recognition technology to identify public threats in an inexpensive manner (no longer needing constant monitoring) and report it to authorities and citizens (via text), which allows first responders to safely address the issue/threat, and reduce the amount of risk exposed to the general public.

The crime rate of the city of Brampton has been on the rise, and there have not been new technologies introduced to address the issue, and ensure the peace in our communities. With the implementation of our voice recognition technology, suspects can be easily apprehended, and criminal activity would be greatly discouraged. Citizens can also be easily notified about a suspected issue/threat (via text) using our program, which greatly reduces the risk exposed to the general public.

Activities/Approach

Create a voice recognition program that recognizes key words and can identify issues/threats from audio recordings based on key words and report the suspect to authorities and notify citizens (via text).
